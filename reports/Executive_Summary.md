# Executive Summary

## Project

System Capacity & Care Load Analytics for Unaccompanied Children

## Purpose

To evaluate healthcare system load, intake pressure, and capacity stress within the UAC Program.

## Key Outcomes

* Developed an interactive analytics dashboard.
* Measured total system care load.
* Monitored intake-discharge balance.
* Identified periods of capacity strain.
* Evaluated backlog accumulation trends.

## Operational Benefits

* Improved visibility into care demand.
* Enhanced staffing and shelter planning.
* Supported proactive capacity management.
* Enabled data-driven decision-making.

## Strategic Recommendations

1. Increase staffing during high-load periods.
2. Accelerate sponsor reunification workflows.
3. Monitor backlog growth continuously.
4. Expand predictive capacity planning capabilities.

## Deliverables

* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Healthcare Analytics Dashboard (Streamlit)
* Executive Summary
* Research Paper

## Conclusion

The project provides a scalable analytical framework for monitoring healthcare system performance, supporting humanitarian operations, and improving resource allocation across the UAC care pipeline.
